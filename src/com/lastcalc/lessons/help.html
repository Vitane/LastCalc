Getting Started

Basic Math

LastCalc let's you do basic math, note that '*' means multiply and '/' means divide, for example:
18/2 + 5 * 2

Brackets

LastCalc will multiply and divide numbers before it adds or subtracts them, but you can force it to calculate part of an answer first by putting it in brackets:
18/(2 + 5) * 2

Basic Conversions

LastCalc understands lots of ways to measure things, weights, distances, durations, and so on. LastCalc will let you convert between different units, some examples:
3 weeks in days
15 km in miles
Or even currency conversions (using up-to-date conversion rates):
$23 in Euros
